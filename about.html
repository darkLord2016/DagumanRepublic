The Daguman Heritage

Our Story

Originating from the northern highlands, the Daguman Republic traces its roots to visionary leaders who cultivated unity, justice, and community. The First Generation — Salvador Daguam, Manuel Daguman, Cayetano Daguman, Timoteo Daguman, Lucas Daguman, and Teodolo Dag-uman — laid the foundation of resilience and kinship.

"Our roots run deep, our story moves forward."

Through generations, the Daguman name became synonymous with honour. Today, the clan association continues to gather members worldwide, celebrating our shared ancestry, upholding traditions, and forging new paths for generations to come.

The legacy entrusted to us by our ancestors is not merely a name — it is a covenant. As stewards of the Daguman heritage, every member carries forward the spirit of unity, justice, and kinship that defined our forebears.
